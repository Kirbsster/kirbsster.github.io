DESIGN

We like to involve our clients in the creative process. We believe that is really important to listen to what they have to say to achieve a high quality and personalised outcome.

The project will be tailored to the specific needs of our clients to make the space practical as well as interesting.

We do:

  • Feasibility Studies
  • Concept Design
  • Developed Design
  • Technical Design

As well as the architectural design we do landscape design, customized furniture design and corporate design for businesses.

CONSTRUCTION

We manage the construction stage for all the projects we develop and drive the build forward efficiently until we handover to the clients.

We are experienced in new builds, remodelling, extensions and refurbishments for residential buildings, retail units and public facilities.

We coordinate effectively with all parties involved to minimise the time and the cost of the build.If required, we organise and oversee external technical services such as geotechnical reports or topographic studies.

We are happy to contact builders for quotes and advise you on material suppliers.

TECHNICAL SERVICES

We give you technical advise and write up technical certificates needed by the Planning Office.

Examples:

  • Planning Application
  • Design and Access Statement
  • Energy Performance Certificates (EPC)*
  • Change of use Planning Application*
  • Activity License*
  • Technical Building Inspection*

We also produce 2D and 3D drawings of existing buildings. The drawings produced would be submitted in digital format (PDF or CAD) as well as a printed copy in the desired scale.

*These certificates are only available for Spanish Projects.
